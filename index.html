FAQs

What is Frontend Mentor, and how will it help me?

Frontend Mentor offers realistic coding challenges to help developers improve their frontend coding skills with projects in HTML, CSS, and JavaScript. It's suitable for all levels and ideal for portfolio building.

Is Frontend Mentor free?

Yes, Frontend Mentor offers both free and premium tiers. The free tier gives you access to a massive library of challenges, starter assets, and our community platform to get feedback on your solutions.

Can I use Frontend Mentor projects in my portfolio?

Absolutely! Deploying your solutions to platforms like GitHub Pages, Vercel, or Netlify and linking them in your portfolio is highly encouraged. It showcases your capability to build pixel-perfect designs.

How can I get help if I'm stuck on a challenge?

The best place to get help is in the Frontend Mentor community Slack channel or the solution comment sections. You can ask questions, share screenshots, or request code reviews from fellow developers.
